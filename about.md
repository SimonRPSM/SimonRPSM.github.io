I graduated from McGill University with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and have been working in automated test and industrial embedded systems ever since. After some time as an Applications Engineer I moved to a Systems Engineering role and spent quite some time working on and deploying embedded systems for validation and production test. My focus then had been mostly on machine vision and motion control. For the first topic, I have designed systems for industrial manufacturing, from lens selection to algorithm creation. For the second, I have done motor sizing and set up various architectures and control schemes.

More recently my focus has been on software architectures and development practices - what are the processes, frameworks and best practices to write, test and deploy code for validation and production test. 

Through the years I have picked up many things in the world of programming and computer science while developing software as part of my career, and decided to start creating some content to share lessons learnt.

\- Simon Perez Santa Maria
